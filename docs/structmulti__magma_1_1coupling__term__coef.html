Detailed Description

The copling_term follows the Darcy permeability law C=1/a/d^2*mu*phi^(2-b) for lower meltfraction and hindled settling C=1/d^2*mu*phi^(-5)*(1-phi). Here coefficients a, grain size d, coefficients b and the cutting between the two demains needs to be set.
